## Key Takeaways

- Chinook salmon are anadromous: born in fresh water, they migrate to the ocean and return to spawn.
- The health of freshwater streams directly determines the survival of salmon eggs and juveniles.
- Native Nations have managed salmon runs sustainably for thousands of years.
